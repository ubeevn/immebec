About Us

François Senneville

Lawyer

Francois Senneville has completed a Bachelor of laws at the University of Sherbrooke in 2004 and passed the exams of the Bar of Quebec in 2005. After completing a mandatory training as a public prosecutor in Montreal in 2006, Francois went on to practice in criminal and statutory law for a few years in defense at various levels including in appeal. Francois had the chance to work with prominent lawyers. In the following years, Francois extended its practice to family law, civil law, and administrative law with success and has pleaded hundreds of cases in the Canadian courts as shown on Canadian jurisprudence websites.

In 2013, Francois was recruited by a Canadian Immigration law firm located in Montreal to assist with administrative law appeals in immigration. Finally, he was sent to Turkey to assist investors for the Quebec investor’s program with their documentation and during their interview with Quebec officials. He then went to China and Thailand the following years to assist mostly investors willing to immigrate to Canada but also skilled workers and other categories. It is in 2017 that Francois came to Vietnam hired by an Asian service provider to pursue the same duties. Francois has assisted hundreds of investors and skilled workers with success in migrating to Canada. In 2018, Francois took the decision to create his own legal firm in Vietnam to insure efficiency and transparency. He is the one in charge of all the legal aspects; he represents all the clients and reviews all the cases at Immibec. Francois is passionate about travelling and discovering other cultures as he has been exposed to many cultures at a young age in Quebec. As a matter of fact, he is fluent in French and English, and can converse in Spanish, Turkish, Thai and Vietnamese.
